About me

A story of good

2018 was the year I was introduced to cryptocurrency. Starting as a beginner, I learned about how cryptocurrency works. In that year, there was a trend of mining and ICOs, which made me interested in learning about it so that I could analyze to find out crypto projects that have potential or rug pulls from tokenomics and smart contracts (token flow).

My journey began with a strong determination to understand crypto projects in various narratives. Through self-learning and actively engaging in crypto projects to build a networking, I gained invaluable knowledge and experience. I learned about community building, collaboration, strategy development, and project analysis from fundamental techniques. These skills allowed me to make the right decisions and made it easier for me to invest in cryptocurrencies.

In the end, I decided to work in the crypto industry in various positions to experience being part of a crypto project team and build a strong network in the industry. 6 years have passed; it was not an easy journey to be in this situation. I believe that with determination and strong goals, anyone can realize their dreams. I am looking forward to the future and an exciting new chapter in my career journey.
